Okay , Here's Your Recommended Activities!

Do Some Deep Breathing

Deep breathing is one of the best ways to lower stress in your body, as it sends a message to your brain to calm down. Try breathing in and out, deeply, for a few minutes, and feel the difference!

All Done!
Have Some Chocolate

Chocolate, specifically dark chocolate, is scientifically proven to help reduce symptoms of stress and anxiety. Reward yourself with some chocolate, you've earned it!

All Done!
Create/Update a Task Planner

One of the main contributions to stress are the things you have to do. Putting everything you need to get done into a planner can make the week seem do-able!

All Done!
